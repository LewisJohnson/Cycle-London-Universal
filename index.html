Cycle London

App

Cycle London is a new app to help you get around quicker. The Santander Cycles scheme is great, but not if you don't know where to get one! This app will tell you where to find one.

About

This app was made for a Hackathon over at Programmr, Which was sponsored by Hired. I would like to thank both of those companies, because otherwise this app wouldn't have existed. I have never made an app before, or anything with a meaningful UI. Without those awesome guys, I would have never tried to make one.

Windows 10

It's not even here and I love it. This app will be ported to Windows 10 in due time with upgrades! In the video I showed off a preview of what's to come. The content scales to the width and height of the window.

Github
